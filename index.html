Our Story

A neighborhood barbershop, the old-fashioned way

Founded in 1978 by Ramon Betancourt, Raymond's has been cutting hair in North Park for generations.

This is an old-school family barbershop delivering quality haircuts of all styles for every hair type. Every cut is finished the traditional way, with hot lather and a straight-razor cleanup on the sideburns and neck. The shop is family-friendly and kids are always welcome.

Two locations to serve you: the original North Park shop (143 Yelp reviews) and a second location in Santee at 225 Town Center Pkwy (160 Yelp reviews).

Good To Know

Frequently Asked Questions

A few honest answers before your visit.

Do I need an appointment?+

Walk-ins are welcome. Popular times like Saturday mornings can mean a short wait, so call ahead at (619) 295-9348 if you want to check the line.

What's included with a haircut?+

Every cut is finished the traditional way, with hot lather and a straight-razor cleanup on the neck and sideburns, a signature touch of this shop since it opened in 1978.

Are kids welcome?+

Yes. This is a family-friendly shop that welcomes boys' and kids' cuts alongside seniors' and adult cuts, all in the same chairs.

Is there more than one location?+

Yes, the original North Park shop on University Ave, plus a second location in Santee at 225 Town Center Pkwy.

What are your hours?+

Tuesday through Saturday, 9am to 5pm, with Sunday afternoon hours as well. Closed Mondays. Call the shop if you need to confirm before a holiday.
